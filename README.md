This repository contains selected projects from my Master's in Business Analytics at Gies College of Business at the University of Illinois.

## Projects

### 1. Associated Wholesale Grocers Consulting Project
- Description: This group project involved analysis of sales data for independent grocery stores which purchased wholesale supplies through Associated Wholesale Grocers (AWG). Data for AWG brand products was used to determine the effectiveness of two programs, AIM Autoship and One Source, in promoting the sales and distribution of new items. The raw data is not included for confidentiality reasons.
- Tools: Python, NumPy, Pandas, Matplotlib

### 2. Consumption and Investment Optimization
- Description: This project involved the use of the JAX machine learning library to optimize consumption and investment ratios for an individual over their lifetime. This includes concepts such as diminishing marginal utility, discounting of future utility, simulation of future stock prices, gradient descent, the sigmoid function, and JIT compilation.
- Tools: Python, NumPy, JAX

### 3. Sustainability Dashboard Case Competition
- Description: This group project involved redesigning a dashboard to showcase the accomplishments made by sustainability researchers in the Gies College of Business. The dashboard was transformed into a storytelling tool with a clearer message, cleaner visuals, and more defined paths for different types of users.
- Tools: Python, Altair, Matplotlib

### 4. Workplace Wellness Case Study
- Description: This group project involved causal analysis of public data from the Illinois Workplace Wellness Study (Jones, Molitor, and Reif 2019; Reif et al. 2020) to determine the impact of workplace wellness programs on employee health and healthcare spending.
- Tools: R, Tidyverse, Dplyr

### 5. Yelp Database Management
- Description: This group project involved the creation and querying of a structured transactional database to store public Yelp data. This includes the process of cleaning and normalizing JSON formatted data from Kaggle, implementation of data into an Azure SQL database, and querying of that database to extract business insights involving the restaurant industry.
- Tools: SQL, Azure, Kaggle, Python, Pandas, JSON
